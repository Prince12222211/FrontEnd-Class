Republic Day is a significant national event in India celebrated on January 26th every year. It marks the adoption of the Constitution of India and the transition of the country to a sovereign republic. On this day, the entire nation comes together to honor the values of justice, liberty, equality, and fraternity enshrined in the Constitution. The main celebration takes place in the capital, New Delhi, where a grand parade showcases the diversity and cultural heritage of India. Citizens across the country also participate in various events to express their patriotism and commitment to the democratic principles upheld by the Republic.
